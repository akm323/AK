IRDC Attendance System

An innovative attendance management system developed for the Innovation Research and Development Center (IRDC) at Future University. This solution replaces expensive attendance hardware with a modern, cost-effective mobile application that leverages QR code technology for seamless employee check-ins and check-outs.

Key Features:

  • QR code-based attendance tracking for secure and quick check-ins/outs
  • Real-time attendance monitoring and reporting
  • Automated attendance records with timestamps
  • Location verification to ensure attendance is marked from the workplace

Admin Dashboard:

  • Comprehensive overview of employee attendance statistics
  • Detailed attendance reports with timestamps
  • Export functionality for record-keeping
  • Employee management and access control
